Why this exists

Built by people learning in public.

Roger started this after helping a Sri Lankan driver-guide rebuild his online presence when bookings had dried up. That project showed something important: AI is most useful when it helps ordinary people solve practical problems.

The AI Apprentice is for Roger, Jane, and anyone else who wants to learn AI without pretending to be an expert on day one.
