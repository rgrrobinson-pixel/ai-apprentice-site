Productivity tools

Wispr Flow

Best for speaking rough notes, emails, prompts, and ideas instead of typing. First exercise: dictate a two-minute note about one repetitive weekly task, then ask Claude to turn it into a clear task brief.

Atlas AI browser / ChatGPT Atlas

Best for guided research while browsing. First exercise: compare three competitor websites and ask what each does well, who it targets, and what your own site could learn.

Claude

Best for thinking, writing, planning, summarising, and review. First exercise: ask Claude to interview you with five questions, then create a one-page AI action plan.

Marketing tools

Basic Bananas Marketing Ecosystem App

Best as a strategy home base before generating content. Use it to clarify brand voice, customer segments, marketing activity, and key numbers.

Leonardo AI

Best for campaign images, visual concepts, mood boards, and creative exploration. Check commercial-use terms and review generated details carefully.

Google AI Studio / Veo

Best for prompt experiments and short AI video concepting. Use it to test story ideas before investing in production.

Descript

Best for editing podcasts, interviews, lessons, webinars, and talking-head videos by editing the transcript.

Kapwing

Best for browser-based social video editing: captions, resizing, trimming, templates, and repurposing content for different platforms.

HeyGen

Best for avatar, presenter, multilingual, and digital-twin style videos. Use consent clearly and avoid pretending AI video is a live recording.

Weekly Social Media Content in 10 Minutes

Best treated as a repeatable workflow. Turn one business idea into five posts: educational, story-based, myth-busting, behind-the-scenes, and offer.

Customer service tools

Jotform AI Agents

Best for simple customer service, lead capture, intake forms, FAQs, booking enquiries, and support triage. Always test unusual questions and make human escalation clear.

Suggested teaching order

1Strategy

Start with the Basic Bananas Ecosystem App.

2Capture

Use Wispr Flow to collect rough thinking.

3Develop

Use Claude to shape ideas and plans.

4Research

Use Atlas for guided browsing.

5Create

Use Leonardo, Veo, Descript, Kapwing, or HeyGen.

6Support

Use Jotform AI Agents for enquiries and FAQs.

Important learner note

AI tools change quickly. Features, pricing, availability, and privacy terms should be checked before using any tool for client work, sensitive data, paid advertising, or public claims.
